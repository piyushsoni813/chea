Event

ChEA believes in bringing together the chemical engineering society of the institute. One-day events and annual trips play an integral role in doing so.
ChEA organizes a variety of intellectual as well as entertaining events all-round the year that promote the academic and extra-curricular well-being of the students.

time capsule

The 25 year Journey, is a one of its kind event where a panel of distinguished alumni help and interact with the students to provide them with unparalleled professional experience and also help the chemical engineers of today to diversify and find what drives them.
